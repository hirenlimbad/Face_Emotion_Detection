Vision Beyond

Vision beyond is a computer vision project that aims to develop an application that can find 7 types of sentiment based on faces. It detects faces automatically and finds the sentiment, and it is able to find multiple faces at a time with their emotions.

Angry, Disgust, Fear, Happy, Neutral, Sad, Surprise

Upload an Image

After uploading image you'll be able to see emotions of multiple people are in image. this will helps you in image annotation, some time it detects unwanteted faces you can easily by seeing documentation.

Upload a Video

After uploading video you'll be able to see emotions of multiple people are in video. you can change some code and get overall sentiment of video by seeing face. this will automate your hours of work.
